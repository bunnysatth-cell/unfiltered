This Privacy Policy explains how Unfiltered (“Unfiltered”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our web-based creator tool (the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information from TikTok

When you connect your TikTok account through TikTok’s official authorization (OAuth) process, we may receive limited information permitted by the scopes you approve, such as:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., user ID, display name, profile image)
  • Authorization tokens required to upload or publish content

We do not receive your TikTok password and cannot access information beyond the permissions you grant.

b) Content You Provide

We temporarily process video files, captions, and metadata that you choose to upload or publish through the Service for the sole purpose of delivering the requested functionality.

c) Technical Information

We may collect limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security, debugging, and operational purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Authenticate your account via TikTok OAuth

We do not use your data for advertising or sell personal information.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Unfiltered does not permanently store TikTok credentials or video content beyond what is reasonably necessary to perform the requested actions.

Authorization tokens may be stored securely for as long as required to provide the Service and can be revoked by you at any time through TikTok.
