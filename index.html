Unfiltered

A web-based creator tool for preparing and publishing short-form video content to TikTok.

What Unfiltered Does

Unfiltered helps creators manage the process of uploading and publishing short-form videos to TikTok through a simple web-based workflow.

Users can prepare video content, review captions and metadata, and publish content to their own TikTok accounts using TikTok’s official APIs.

How TikTok Integration Works

  • Users connect their TikTok account using TikTok’s official authorization (OAuth).
  • The permissions requested are limited to what is required for uploading or publishing content.
  • Videos are uploaded or published only when explicitly initiated by the user.
  • Unfiltered does not request or store TikTok passwords.

User Control

Users retain full control over their content and TikTok account at all times. TikTok authorization can be revoked at any time through TikTok account settings.

Important Notes

  • Unfiltered is not affiliated with or endorsed by TikTok.
  • Content performance, reach, and engagement are determined by TikTok and user behavior.
  • Use of the service must comply with TikTok’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
